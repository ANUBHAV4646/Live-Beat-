About Anuv Jain

Anuv Jain is a singer-songwriter whose music touches the hearts of many. With his deeply personal lyrics and soothing melodies, he has created a niche for himself in the indie music scene. His songs, often reflecting on love, loss, and self-discovery, resonate with a young, emotional audience.

His live performances are known for their simplicity and authenticity, with acoustic sets that allow his voice and lyrics to shine. He has gained a dedicated following across India and beyond, making him one of the most beloved indie artists of his generation.

Anuv Jain is excited to announce his upcoming India tour in 2024. The tour will cover various cities, including Delhi, Mumbai, and Bangalore, giving fans an intimate experience of his new music along with beloved hits such as "Baarishein" and "Mishri."
