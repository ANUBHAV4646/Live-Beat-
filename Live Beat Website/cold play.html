About Coldplay

Coldplay is a British rock band formed in 1996, known for their melodic music and poignant lyrics. The band consists of lead vocalist Chris Martin, guitarist Jonny Buckland, bassist Guy Berryman, and drummer Will Champion. Over the years, Coldplay has evolved their sound, incorporating elements from various genres and achieving global acclaim.

They are celebrated for their immersive live performances and have left an indelible mark on the music industry with hits that resonate with audiences worldwide.
