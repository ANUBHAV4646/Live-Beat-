Anuv Jain

Hailing from India, Anuv Jain is taking the music scene by storm with his soulful melodies and heartfelt lyrics. Known for his unique blend of folk and contemporary music, Anuv has captured the hearts of fans around the world. Don’t miss your chance to experience his enchanting performance live!

Karan Aujhla

The Punjabi music sensation Karan Aujhla is set to light up the stage with his powerful vocals and dynamic presence. With numerous chart-topping hits under his belt, Karan has established himself as one of the leading voices in the Punjabi music industry. Join us as he brings his electrifying energy to our concert series!

Diljit Dosanjh

A household name in the Punjabi and Bollywood music scenes, Diljit Dosanjh is a multi-talented artist known for his versatile singing and charismatic performances. From upbeat dance tracks to soulful ballads, Diljit’s music resonates with audiences of all ages. Prepare to be mesmerized by his performance at Live Beat!

Coldplay

Known for their innovative sound and captivating live shows, Coldplay has become a global phenomenon. This is a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity to see them perform their greatest hits live, and we guarantee it will be a night to remember!
