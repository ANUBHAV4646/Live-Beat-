About Diljit Dosanjh

Diljit Dosanjh is one of the most famous and beloved Punjabi artists, known for his captivating voice and powerful performances. From chart-topping songs to iconic roles in movies, Diljit has become a household name globally, and his live performances bring energy and joy to audiences around the world.

His influence spans across music, film, and social causes, making him a true superstar and a role model for many.

Achievements & Popular Songs

Diljit Dosanjh has had an extraordinary career, with numerous chart-topping hits and award-winning performances. Some of his most famous songs include:

  • Do You Know
  • 5 Taara
  • Laembadgini
  • Putt Jatt Da
  • Patiala Peg

Beyond his musical success, Diljit has starred in multiple blockbuster Punjabi films, including Udta Punjab and Honsla Rakh, solidifying his presence in the film industry.

Awards & Recognitions

Diljit Dosanjh has been honored with numerous awards for his contributions to both the music and film industries. His accolades include:

  • Best Playback Singer at the Filmfare Awards (2017)
  • Punjabi Icon Award by PTC Punjabi (2020)
  • Best Actor for Udta Punjab (2017)
  • Multiple Brit Asia TV Music Awards

Ticket Sales & Global Popularity

Diljit's concerts are a spectacle, with tickets selling out within hours. He has a massive global following, with fans from India, Canada, the UK, and Australia flocking to see him live. His recent achievements include:

  • Sold-out shows at London's Wembley Arena
  • Record-breaking ticket sales in Canada and Australia
  • First Indian artist to perform at the Coachella Valley Music and Arts Festival in 2023

Diljit continues to be a top draw at festivals worldwide, captivating fans with his energetic performances and genuine love for his audience.
